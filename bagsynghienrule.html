Bagsynghien Rule - Romanization Rule (로마자 표기법 혹은 전사법)

While being inspired by the the earlier works of Fukui Rei and others, Bagsynghien Rule takes care of modern day application of romanization rule.

The bottom line is there should be one-on-one correlation between letters of Hangul and Roman Alphabet.
